Our Story

Founded in 2010, Rishta.com has become India's leading matrimonial service. We started with a simple mission: to help people find their perfect life partners through a trusted and secure platform.

Over the years, we've successfully matched thousands of couples who are now happily married. Our unique compatibility algorithm and personalized matchmaking services set us apart in the industry.

We understand the importance of cultural values, family expectations, and personal preferences in marriage, which is why we've designed our service to cater specifically to Indian families.
